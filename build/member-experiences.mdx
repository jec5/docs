---
title: "Member Experiences"
---

Through [Fielo Loyalty API](/api-reference), it's easy to surface loyalty data on the touchpoints your members already use to interact with your brand. Take a look at this example of a member experience embedded into a mobile app.

### Overview and Available Incentives

Members see their current balance(s), tier and the incentives available to them based on their segments.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Home Member Overview And Incentives List](/images/mobile-app-Home-Member-Overview-and-Incentives-list.png)
</Frame>

When selecting an incentive, members can check out what they'll earn and any eligibility details.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Incentive Details](/images/mobile-app-incentive-details.png)
</Frame>

### Profile and Tiers

Members can verify and update their profile info, as well as view their tier status, benefits and learn how to upgrade.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Profile And Membership Levels](/images/mobile-app-profile-and-membership-levels.png)
</Frame>

### Rewards Catalog

Members view their available balances and browse reward options in the catalog.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Rewards Catalog Empty Cart](/images/mobile-app-rewards-catalog-empty-cart.png)
</Frame>

Upon selecting a reward, they can see its price and description and add it to their cart.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Reward Details](/images/mobile-app-reward-details.png)
</Frame>

Once the desired items are added, members proceed to checkout.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Checkout](/images/mobile-app-checkout.png)
</Frame>

After completing checkout, an order confirmation is displayed.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App Order Confirmed](/images/mobile-app-order-confirmed.png)
</Frame>

Members can then view the details of their redemptions at any time on the My Redemptions page, accessible from the catalog.

<Frame>
  ![Mobile App My Redemptions](/images/mobile-app-my-redemptions.png)
</Frame>
